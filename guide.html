What is ETH?
To buy MPX, you first need ETH. Think of ETH as the fuel that powers crypto transactions.
Ξ
Ethereum
ETH · The fuel of crypto
ETH is the most widely used crypto after Bitcoin. You need a small amount to pay for transactions (called "gas fees"). On Base network, fees are less than $0.10.
💡 You only need $10–15 of ETH. A small part covers fees (~$0.10), the rest becomes MPX.
