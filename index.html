What is Metriplex

Identity built on
geometry

🔷
Your key is a shape

In Bitcoin and Ethereum your identity is a 256-bit integer. In Metriplex your private key is an Iterated Function System — a set of geometric contractions that define a unique fractal attractor.

🔬
ZK proof by geometry

Every transaction includes a zero-knowledge proof that your attractor satisfies 8 geometric criteria simultaneously. Forgery requires solving the Inverse IFS Problem — conjectured NP-hard.

🌉
Native bridge to Ethereum

MPX tokens live on Base mainnet. The cross-chain relayer monitors both chains — send MPX to receive wMPX on Ethereum, or burn wMPX to release MPX natively.
